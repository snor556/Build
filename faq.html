General

What is rainwater harvesting?

Rainwater harvesting is the collection and storage of rainwater for later use. It involves collecting rainwater from rooftops and storing it in tanks or other containers. This water can then be used for various purposes, such as irrigation, toilet flushing, and even drinking after proper treatment.

How does AquaHarvest work?

AquaHarvest is a comprehensive platform that simplifies rainwater harvesting. Our app allows you to calculate potential savings, visualize systems with AR, find local aquifers, and connect with a community of enthusiasts and experts.

Is rainwater safe to drink?

Raw rainwater collected from most surfaces is not recommended for drinking without treatment. It can contain contaminants from the air and your roof. Proper filtration and purification are necessary to make it potable. AquaHarvest provides information on appropriate treatment methods.

AquaHarvest App

How do I calculate my potential water savings?

AquaHarvest uses your rooftop size, average rainfall data for your location, and your water usage patterns to estimate your potential water savings. Simply enter your details in the app, and it will provide you with a personalized estimate.

How does the AR visualization feature work?

Our AR feature uses your phone's camera to overlay 3D models of rainwater harvesting systems onto your property. This helps you visualize how different tanks and components will look and fit before you make a purchase.
