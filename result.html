Recommended Harvesting Methods

roofing

Rain Barrels

Simple and affordable, rain barrels connect to your downspouts to collect water for gardening and other non-potable uses. Ideal for smaller roofs and locations with moderate rainfall.

Potential Benefits:

  • Reduces water bills
  • Provides chlorine-free water for plants
  • Easy to install and maintain
aod

Dry System

A dry system uses pipes that drain directly into a storage tank. This method is effective for larger roofs and areas with infrequent but heavy rainfall. The pipes remain dry between rain events.

Potential Benefits:

  • Larger water storage capacity
  • Lower risk of mosquito breeding
  • Can be integrated with underground cisterns
